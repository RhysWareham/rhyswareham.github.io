The symbol reveals, and then nudges for each spin. Expander symbols can land and increase the size of the symbol to 7x7. The symbol nudges until reaching the bottom row)</li><li>Multiplier symbols (Increase the overall multiplier during the Colossal Nudging Feature)</li><li>Collector Symbols</li><li>Multiplier Symbols</li><li>Instant Coins (instant prize of bet multiplied by number on coin can land inside the colossal mystery symbols)</li><li>Recovery (of game at any stage should the player return)</li></ul>
